About Jenna

Her Journey

2020

During the pandemic, Jenna had a lot of free time. To fill the vacant time, she began to cook many dishes. This led her to experiment with a multitude of combinations. From these, a few suprised her causing her to post about them.

2022

After creating and publishing dishes she was proud of, she began to become more popular among many homecooks looking to try something different. With support from many homecooks, and curious individuals, she started to become a name you would hear very often, showing she has now gained some traction. Her popularity led to here creating more recipes.

2024

After posting more recipes, she continued to gain support and eventually wanted to reach more people.She started making youtube tutorials and even got featured on a TV show.

2026

Due to the influx of contact from viewers and fanns requesting more recipes and what to use specifically, Jenna eventually wanted to make her recipes always available, which is why she contacted for a website to be built for her customers.
